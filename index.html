About Mikki Lens

Mikki Lens is an Afrobeat singer, songwriter, and photographer from Benue State, Nigeria, whose music blends soulful melodies with the vibrant pulse of African rhythm.

Rooted in everyday stories of love, growth, and identity, his sound bridges traditional Afrobeat energy with modern emotion. A guitarist and storyteller at heart, Mikki Lens channels his experiences into songs that speak to the dreams and struggles of a new generation.
